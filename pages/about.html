About Us

Two specialists.
One shared mission.

Ivy Learning Lab is a Dublin-based learning consultancy founded by Oisín and Katherine — two senior practitioners who believe that exceptional learning requires both the science of instructional design and the discipline of operational excellence.

The Team

People who've done the work

O
Oisín
Senior Instructional Design Consultant

Oisín is a senior instructional design consultant with over 15 years of experience helping organisations transform subject-matter expertise into high-impact, results-driven learning. With a PhD in Education from Dublin City University specialising in blended and technology-enhanced learning, he combines academic rigour with practical execution to design scalable, learner-centred programmes aligned to strategic business goals.

His career spans international teaching — including a Fulbright award — large-scale digital initiatives such as Duolingo's Irish course, and senior instructional design roles. Today, he partners with organisations to design learning that is practical, measurable, and built to deliver lasting impact.

Our Mission

Building learning that lasts

Our mission is to help eLearning teams work smarter — by building streamlined workflows, integrating AI with purpose, and creating systems that support better collaboration, faster delivery, and stronger learning outcomes.

We focus on the people behind the process, designing solutions that remove friction and let teams focus on what they do best: creating impactful learning.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every team has its own tools, challenges, and ways of working. That's why we take the time to understand your specific context before recommending anything.
